- **New Period Summary Component**: A new component, `PeriodSummary`, has been introduced to display
  a detailed financial summary for each period, including income, expense, delta, and cumulative
  balance.
- **Multi-Currency Delta Breakdown**: The `PeriodSummary` component now provides a breakdown of the
  delta by individual currencies, offering more granular insights into financial movements.
